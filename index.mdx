## Why Auditoriapp?

Auditoriapp replaces opaque Excel spreadsheets and PDF-based workflows with a centralized digital platform that ensures data isolation between communities and provides real-time oversight for auditors.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Audit Workflow & Transparency">
    Complete audit trail from budget allocation to expense justification. Auditors have cross-community visibility to validate all financial movements with attached evidence and documentation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Document Management">
    Upload and validate receipts, invoices, and evidence for every expense. Documents are linked directly to expenses and projects for seamless audit review.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Real-Time Analytics">
    Interactive dashboards with KPIs showing budget execution, project status, and financial health across all communities or individual tenants.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Period-Based Budgets">
    Organize funding in discrete periods with allocated budgets, carry-forward balances, and time-bound project execution tracking.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
